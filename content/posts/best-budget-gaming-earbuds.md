## 1. Razer Hammerhead X

- **Price:** ~$50
- **Connection:** USB-C
- **Pros:** Low latency mode, good sound, includes USB-C to 3.5mm adapter
- **Cons:** Short cable

Best all-around gaming earbuds with low latency for competitive gaming.
